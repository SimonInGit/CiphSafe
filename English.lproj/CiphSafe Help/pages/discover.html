Discover CiphSafe

CiphSafe provides an easy-to-use method for storing account+password pairs as well as any sort of notes you wish to keep safe. The notes section of each entry is basically a full-blown RTFD editor, just like TextEdit.

Adding and editing entries

Adding and changing entries is quite simple, done with a no-nonsense entry window. Viewing and/or changing is as simple as double-clicking an entry.

Searching is simple, Mac OS X-style

Searching entries in CiphSafe is as easy as the familiar search in other Mac OS X applications: click in the search field in the upper-right corner and start typing. Click the magnifier glass to select which field to search, and the list of entries is constantly updated with your search needs.

Exporting data

CiphSafe now has two ways to get data to other applications: drag and drop, and an export option (new in 1.3). Drag and drop works as any Mac user would expect, and use the export feature to generate CSV or XML files from your data.
